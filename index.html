About Casper

Casper is a proof-of-stake blockchain network optimized for enterprise and developer adoption. It aims to accelerate business and innovation by making blockchain technology more accessible and user-friendly for businesses and developers.

Key features of Casper include:

  • High throughput and low gas fees
  • Upgradeable smart contracts
  • Developer-friendly environment with WebAssembly support
  • Energy-efficient proof-of-stake consensus mechanism

Delegating Coins

Delegating your CSPR tokens allows you to participate in network security and earn rewards. Here's how to delegate using the Casper wallet:

  1. Open your Casper Wallet and navigate to the Staking page.
  2. Click on the "Delegate" button.
  3. Choose a validator from the list or enter a validator's public key.
  4. Enter the amount of CSPR you want to delegate.
  5. Review the transaction details and confirm.
  6. Sign the transaction using your connected wallet.

Note: There is an unbonding period of 7 eras (approximately 14 hours) when you decide to undelegate your tokens.

For more detailed information, visit Casper Delegation Guide.

Undelegating Coins

To undelegate your CSPR tokens:

  1. Open your Casper Wallet and go to the Staking page.
  2. Find the validator you've delegated to and click "Undelegate".
  3. Enter the amount of CSPR you want to undelegate.
  4. Review the transaction details and confirm.
  5. Sign the transaction using your connected wallet.

Important: After undelegating, your tokens will be locked for 7 eras before becoming available in your wallet.

For more detailed information, visit Casper Undelegation Guide.
